Introduction

Hello, my name is Angel Castro. This website is part of my web development coursework and demonstrates basic HTML structure without using CSS or external frameworks. The site contains three pages: a welcome page, a resume page, and a page describing some of my personal interests.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Technology through the Illinois Institute of Technology online program offered through Coursera. I previously completed an associate degree and graduated from high school.

  • A future resume outlining education and expected skills
